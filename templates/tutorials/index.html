FAQ

Got questions about BusView? We've got answers! Browse our frequently asked questions below to learn more about how to use our platform and make the most of your public transit experience.

What is BusView?

BusView is a platform designed to simplify public transportation by offering real-time bus stop information, trip planning, and an interactive map to help you navigate with ease.

Is emulated works for all buses?

For all except Paphos buses. In future Paphos buees will be added.

Why I can't use 'info' button for Paphos buses?

As with emulation, this function not work only for Paphos buses, in future it will be added.

Is emulation is accurate?

No, it's just a function which will show you, where buses must be based on official schedule. ITS NOT REAL BUS LOCATION

What is 'TT Bus'?

TT Bus - TimeTable Bus. This is bus which must arrive to this stop at specified time, which is taken from the timetable. It is showing when no real bus before this stop

How do I plan a trip?

Navigate to the Plan Trip section, enter your starting point and destination, and BusView will provide you with the best routes and schedules based on real-time data.

Can I save my trips?

Yes! In the Saved Trips section, you can store your frequently used routes for quick access anytime.

Is BusView available on mobile devices?

BusView is fully responsive and works seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices. We're also working on dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android!

How accurate is the real-time information?

We source our data from government and update it in real-time to ensure accuracy. However, delays (+-10 seconds) may occur due to unforeseen circumstances.
