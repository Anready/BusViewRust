About BusView

Welcome to BusView, your ultimate companion for navigating public transportation with ease and confidence. Our mission is to make your journey seamless by providing real-time bus stop information, trip planning, and an intuitive map experience.

Founded in 2024, BusView was created with a passion for improving urban mobility. Whether you're a daily commuter or an occasional traveler, we're here to help you find your way, one stop at a time.
